Alpine.

The Alpine map in Free Fire immerses players in a winter wonderland filled with snow-covered landscapes and icy terrains. This map offers a refreshing contrast to the more tropical and urban settings of other maps, providing a unique and challenging environment for combat. Key locations such as the Ice Castle, Snowfall, and Militia Camp are strategically scattered, each offering distinct advantages and opportunities for tactical gameplay. The map's varying elevations, from snow-capped mountains to frozen lakes, demand players to be vigilant and adaptive, using the terrain to gain strategic upper hands. The Alpine map's blend of serene beauty and treacherous landscapes makes it a thrilling playground where survival skills and strategic thinking are put to the test.
